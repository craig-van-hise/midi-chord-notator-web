### 3. Workflows (Macros)

**Purpose:**
Workflows are **user-triggered** sequences. They act like saved prompts or "macros" that you invoke explicitly (usually via a slash command like `/test` or `/deploy`). They are best for repeatable, multi-step processes specific to the repository.

* **Format:** Markdown (`.md`) with a specific structure including YAML frontmatter and standard sections.
* **How it works:** When you type the trigger, the agent executes the steps defined in the workflow file.
* **Project Path:** `<project_root>/.agent/workflows/` (Useful for repo-specific tasks like `database-migration.md`).

**Workflow Schema Template:**

```markdown
---
command: /<command-name>
description: "<Description of the workflow>"
permissions:
  terminal: <read|write>
  filesystem: <read|write>
---

# Agent Persona
[Description of the agent's role and mindset for this task]

# Execution Standard
- **[Standard Name]:** [Description of constraints or behavior]
- **[Standard Name]:** [Description]

# Workflow Steps

1. **[Step 1 Name]**
   - [Instruction]

2. **[Step 2 Name]**
   - [Instruction]

# Output
1. [Expected output 1]
2. [Expected output 2]


```
